### Requirements for Participants

- A **working laptop** (Windows, Mac, or Linux) is required for coding.  
- A **power charger** and **internet access** are essential.  
- **Google Chrome** is recommended for Google Cloud tools and labs.  
- A personal **Gmail account** is needed to access Google Cloud / AI Studio.  
- A **GitHub account** is nice to have.  
- **Pre-installed tools** may be needed (will be communicated later), depending on the session.  
- **Basic knowledge of Python or API tools** may be helpful.  
- **Basic Python programming knowledge** is recommended.  
- Knowledge of how to **send API requests and handle responses** is helpful.  
- A basic understanding of **generative AI and prompt engineering** is helpful.
